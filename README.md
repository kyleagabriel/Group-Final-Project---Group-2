# Group-Final-Project---Group-2

Business Concept:
Pitstop.ph is a modern, community-driven automotive marketplace built for Filipino car owners, sellers, and service providers. The platform simplifies the end-to-end car parts buying journey â€” from product discovery and part compatability, all the way to booking installation services.

Tech Stack: Django 5.2.8

Deployment Link: [https://kylegabriel.pythonanywhere.com/products/](https://kylegabriel.pythonanywhere.com/accounts/login/)

Setup/Run Instruction:
1. sign up an account. One (1) account per role type.
2. As the Seller, input as much products as you want. Logout afterwards
3. As the Customer, input your car in the filter bar and save it.
4. As the Customer, purchase any products that you want. Book an installation appointment afterwards.
5. As the Customer, you can check your historical transactions to track delivery.
6. As the Installer, accept or reject the booking application.

Performance of the webapp is much more complete in a sense that it is an end to end webapp for all 3 stakeholders. Customer can go all the way to checkout, while also tracking delivery. Seller and Installer have a main dashboard with simple analytics as well as their own necessary functions for their business operations.
